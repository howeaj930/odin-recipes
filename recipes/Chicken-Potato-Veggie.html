Chicken - Potato - Veggie

The trio dish of Chicken, Potato, and Veggie is a Tim classic and a legendary dish that continues to withstand the test of time. Enjoyed historicaly for many years, and to continue its prosporious existance for many years to come.

This dish is easy to enjoy (except the potatoes sometimes), and even easier to make!
